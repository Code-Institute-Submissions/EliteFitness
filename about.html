About Us

At Elite Fitness e specialise in small group personal training. We are not like other gyms where you go to a class of over 30 people and get no attention from the coach, on average we have around six per session. Each session is tailored to your own level from beginner to advanced.

Each of our coaches will know you by name and know what your goals are. Whether your goal is to get your first pull up or compete in a body building competition, we are here to help you every step of the way.

Each session is tailored from beginner to advanced and our highly skilled coaches can help if you need an alternative exercise or are returning after an injury.

Our members get nutritonal guides, nutritonal coaching and recipe books.

We use Inbody scans showing you your muscle mass and body fat percentage and do these regularly to track your progess so you know where you are on your journey.
